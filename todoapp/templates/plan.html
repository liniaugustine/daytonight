Pathiri

Appa is also called Pathiri in Malabar cooking. These are essentially thin rice rotis served hot. Brown rice is high in fiber, acts as an anti-oxidant and has a low glycemic index. It helps in preventing diabetes, weight loss, heart disease and lowers cholesterol due to its high fiber content.
